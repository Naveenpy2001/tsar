Overview

Social media platforms have become integral to communication and information sharing in the digital age. Platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram offer users a space to connect, share updates, and engage with a global audience

E-commerce platforms have revolutionized the way businesses sell and consumers shop. Marketplaces like Amazon, eBay, and Shopify provide a digital space for businesses to showcase and sell products globally.

Collaboration platforms and project management tools have become essential for remote work and team efficiency. Platforms such as Slack, Microsoft Teams, and Trello facilitate communication, file sharing, and project coordination among team members, irrespective of geographical locations.
